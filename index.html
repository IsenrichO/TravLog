Interactive Cards

  • Organize your past travels in card-like format.
  • Or do the same for a trip you want to take.
  • Associate with high-quality vector graphic maps and flags!

Google Maps

  • Generate a visual layer to show where you've been.
  • Choose from the default Styled Map, Google's standard style and more.
  • Get a real-time view of where you've gone and where you want to go!

Info-Windows

  • Get interesting location data for the world.
  • Interact with Google Fusion Tables data.
  • Learn more about the world we live in!

Make An Itinerary
Plan For Future Expeditions

  • Simply looking to make a record of a trip you're thinking of taking? Then you're where you need to be!
  • Select international destinations and create organized layouts of your planned voyages.
  • Interact with the most up-to-date mapping data on the side!

Record Your Travel Experiences

  • Wax nostalgic and reflect on the adventures you've had with a concrete record of your trips!
  • Use simple point-and-click methods to interact and record the places to which you've been!
  • Share with friends and family. Have fun reliving your days abroad.
